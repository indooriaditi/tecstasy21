CAPTURE THE FLAG


It consists of two rounds.

Round 1:

Trivia (Quiz)
Questions asked will be related to cryptography & cybersecurity. Participants will be filtered based on cutoff score.



Round 2:

Capture the Flag
Participants be provided details of a bad actor. They need to escalate their privilages using website & human Vulnerabilities and exploit him. Submit the flag you find. This is a time based round, the one who finds the flag first will be awarded.

Top 3 participants to complete this in least amount of time will be rewarded.
