TECHIE CONNEXION


Techie Connexion consists of two rounds.

Round 1:

Pictionary
In this round, a set of pictures will be displayed on the screen. The pictures put together form a technical word which has to be guessed.



Round 2:

Escape Room
A team will be locked up in a virtual room. With the help of clues, by solving logical/ technical questions, the team has to escape from the room. The team has to accomplish the tasks in a limited amount of time. After cracking the first clue, the next clues will be revealed.
