TECH VIRUS


Tech Virus has three rounds.

Round 1:

Tell me 5
In this round, the participants will be divided into groups and asked questions. The participants should name any 5 words related to the question.



Round 2:

Word Chain
It is similar to what we call word building, i.e., come up with a new word based on the last word's last letter. The participants will be divided into groups and will have to play the game only with technical terms. If participants cannot answer 3 times, they're out of the game.



Round 3:

Jumbled Pattern
The code for a series or pattern is provided with a few lines jumbled. The participants have to rearrange them to get the correct output.
