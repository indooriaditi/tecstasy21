TIC TECH TOE


Have you ever wondered if you can play Tic Tac Toe and test your Technical Knowledge both at the same time? Gear up then!

Round 1:

This round is similar to classic “Tic Tac Toe” game, but the player can choose the marking position only if he/she can answer the technical question in the given time. The faster you answer, the more you score.



Round 2:

This round is similar to classic “Connect 4” game, the top scorers of round one are qualified to play this round. The rules are similar to round one.
