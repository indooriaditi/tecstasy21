BRAIN BOOSTERS


Brain Boosters has 3 rounds.

Round 1:

There will be MCQs from programming languages C, C++, Java.



Round 2:

A problem statement will be given to the participants and the logic for the problem should be given. The one that comes up with the best logic will be selected for next round.



Round 3:

It's a rapid fire round where the first person to answer correctly will be rewarded points.
