TECH FUN


This event consists of three rounds.

Round 1:

Memory Game
Players need to match similar elements by turning over two cards at a time, with the goal of turning a matching pair by using their memory.



Round 2:

Picture Puzzle
Picture puzzle is a Jigsaw game where players have to put the pieces to the right places. Players have to complete the puzzle within limited time.



Round 3:

Word search
It is a word game that consists of the letters of technical words placed in a grid, which usually has a rectangular or square shape. The objective of this puzzle is to find and mark all the words hidden inside the box.



Top 3 who have the highest score will be awarded.
